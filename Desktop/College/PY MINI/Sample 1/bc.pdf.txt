more complicated to grasp how it works than it appears. Time travel is a concept that we, as
humans, are unfamiliar with. It's also the most contentious issue. There are numerous sites
dedicated to time travel that inform us based on scientific notions. So, throughout this blog,
I've talked about what I consider to be time travel.

things that have happened to us might be changed. I wanted to see how dinosaurs looked in
real life. I would have spent so much time in the time machine that it would have transported
